From a Small Farm to Your Table

It all started in 2020 when our founder, inspired by a trip through the forests of the Pacific Northwest, decided to bring the magic of wild mushrooms to everyday kitchens.

What began as a small hobby farm in a garage has grown into a full-scale organic mushroom operation, supplying hundreds of families, restaurants, and chefs across the country.

Every mushroom we grow is a labour of love — carefully cultivated in climate-controlled environments, harvested by hand, and shipped within 24 hours to ensure you get the freshest product possible.

Years of Growing

Our Journey

How We Grew

🌱
2020

The Beginning

Started growing Pearl Oyster mushrooms in a small garage setup with just 10 grow bags.

🏠
2021

First Farm Space

Moved to a dedicated 500 sq ft grow room and expanded to 5 varieties including Golden and Pink Oyster.

🚚
2022

Online Store Launch

Launched our online store and began delivering fresh mushrooms directly to customers' doors.

🏆
2023

Award Winning

Won the Regional Organic Produce Award and partnered with 20+ local restaurants.

🌍
2024–Now

Growing Nationwide

Expanded to a 3,000 sq ft facility, shipping to customers across the entire country.
